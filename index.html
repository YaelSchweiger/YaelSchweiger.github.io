Hi. This is me.

Nice to meet you, Yael Schweiger.

I am ideas architect. My passions in life were always understanding, verbalising and making visions come true, whether it's me that's actually doing them, or helping others to do so.
I have passion for beautiful, smart and elegant things: it could be a smart logo design or a surprising building architecture.
I'm a people person.
I love getting my hands dirty and creating something new by myself, no matter how much work it takes.

My best self is when I am doing a project (of any kind) with other people - the interaction, the creation and the problem solving - they all move me and make my days and nights filled with passion and doing-atmosphere.
